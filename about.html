Company background

AIM Ultramaster Sdn Bhd. was established in 2015 which is a trusted supplier to demanding customers in the computer peripheral, semiconductor, automotive, can maker, electronics and others industries. We have invested in some of the most sophisticated engineering machinery & equipment available today which our team involved highly technical & motivated people with the skills and experience to produce complex components for critical applications to the finest tolerances. Our factory at Penang in Malaysia has the flexibility and capability to handle one-off prototypes or reverse engineering design and produce using the latest technology equipment for fast turn-round and competitive pricing. Our engineering design team uses sophisticated solid works to develop 3D drawings that are optimised for economical production with considerable experience in jig & fixture, die set and automation project with variable of machine applications. We work closely with customer's project teams to provide with effectiveness solution that balances the right level of performance and precision with economical process costs. We back that investment with sophisticated production engineering and management systems that enable us to plan and control process overflow by meet with most challenging globalize commitment.
